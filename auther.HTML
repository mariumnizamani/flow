Awaking in the Sleeping World

by Marium

"Awaking in the Sleeping World" invites you on a transformative journey of self-discovery and purpose. Through the misty paths of life's uncertainties, this book illuminates the way toward inner peace and fulfillment.

What you'll discover:

  • How to break free from life's repetitive cycles and find deeper meaning
  • The power of gratitude and finding peace in simplicity
  • Practical wisdom for navigating the challenges of modern life
  • How to transform regret into growth and purpose
  • The art of living with intention and awareness

Marium's gentle, reflective storytelling blends warmth with wisdom, guiding readers to embrace every step of the journey with grace and resilience.

Genre: Self-Help / Personal Growth / Inspirational

Pages: 100

Format: Paperback, eBook

About the Author

Marium is a passionate writer and seeker of life's deeper meaning. Since childhood, she has been fascinated by the human experience and the search for purpose.

She began writing this book after gaining significant life experience and understanding the reality of stepping into something new. Her writing is inspired by personal reflections, observations of the world around her, and a deep desire to help others find meaning in their own journeys.

Marium writes to inspire, to comfort, and to spark the light within each reader. She believes that if her words help even one person understand themselves better, the purpose of her writing is fulfilled.

When not writing, Marium enjoys exploring new ideas, connecting with people from different walks of life, and continuously learning from the greatest teacher of all - life itself.
